          Calculations Disclaimer

          Calculations Disclaimer

          Tables in this page can contain calculations that are not verified and may contain errors.
          Calculations are described in the table header descriptions.
          Inputs for calculations are marked in bold text and may be cleared by entering blank input.

          Unaltered Routes (no bold values) displayed in the Active Route (top/left table)
          contain values directly derived from the original Mission Binder and does not have any calculations.
          Unaltered Routes can be accessed by selecting from the Route List menu.
          The primary route from a new mission plan load is also unaltered.

          Calculation Dependencies

          [WIND INPUT] --> GS --> LEG TIME --> ETA --> Contingency ETA
                                 --> TTG
                                 --> LEG FUEL --> FUEL --> Contingency FUEL

[TAS INPUT]  --> GS --> LEG TIME --> ETA --> Contingency ETA
                                 --> TTG
                                 --> LEG FUEL --> FUEL --> Contingency FUEL

[ETA INPUT]  --> ETA --> Contingency ETA

[PPH INPUT]  --> LEG FUEL --> FUEL --> Contingency FUEL

[FUEL INPUT] --> FUEL --> Contingency FUEL
